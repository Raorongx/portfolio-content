Introduction to my hometown Shenzhen

Most people in the west have heard of Hong Kong, but few know the city Shenzhen, which is a economic special zone in China located right next to Hong Kong and had a GDP of about $400bn in 2021, also hosting more than 100 ipo or pre-ipo firms of China. In my website, I'm going to lead you to explore some of the most basic aspects of this mesmerizing Chinese city.

The website will be divided into four sections: In the main section, you will see basic introduction of this city. In the history section, I'm goin to show you the brief history of this city and how it became the first tier city of China in just 40 yeas. In the current section, you will see what the city is like now compared to what it was 40 years ago. In the gallery section, you will see the beauty of this city by browsing through some of its images.
